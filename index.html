Meet Our CEO

Lindiwe Mashamba

Founder & Clinical Psychologist

With over 15 years of experience in mental health and organizational development, Lindiwe Mashamba has dedicated her career to improving mental health services in South Africa. She holds a Master's degree in Clinical Psychology and has worked extensively with both individuals and organizations.

Her expertise includes trauma counseling, emotional intelligence training, and organizational development. Lindiwe is passionate about making mental health services accessible to all communities and has been instrumental in developing several community-based mental health programs.

Key Qualifications:

  • Master's in Clinical Psychology
  • Certified Trauma Specialist
  • Organizational Development Consultant
  • Emotional Intelligence Trainer
